Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about StudyRabbit.

Is StudyRabbit free to use?

Yes! StudyRabbit is completely free with all features included. We believe in making productivity tools accessible to all students.

Does StudyRabbit work with other browsers?

Currently, StudyRabbit is available exclusively for Google Chrome. We're working on versions for Firefox and Edge in the future.

Is my study data private?

Absolutely! All your study data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or transmit any personal information or study statistics.

Can I customize the websites that get blocked?

Yes, StudyRabbit allows you to create a personalized list of distracting websites to block during focus mode. You can easily edit this list in the settings.

How do I export my study data?

In the extension's options page, you'll find data management tools that allow you to export all your study data and settings in JSON format, which you can later import if needed.
